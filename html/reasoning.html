Logical Reasoning Handbook

Number Series

Definition: A sequence of numbers arranged in a specific order based on a pattern.

Types: Arithmetic Series, Geometric Series, Fibonacci Series, Prime Numbers.

Example: Identify the missing number in the sequence: 2, 6, 12, 20, __, 42.
Solution: The pattern follows n² + n. Answer: 30.

Blood Relations

Definition: Understanding family relationships based on given conditions.

Common Relations: Parent-Child, Sibling, Grandparents, Uncle-Aunt, Cousins.

Example: A is the father of B. B is the mother of C. What is A to C?
Solution: A is C’s grandfather.

Coding-Decoding

Definition: The method of encrypting words or numbers into a different format based on a logical pattern.

Types: Letter shifting, Reverse Order, Number Coding.

Example: If CAT = DBU, what is DOG?
Solution: Each letter is shifted by 1. Answer: EPH.

Ages

Definition: Problems involving age calculations based on present, past, or future conditions.

Example: A is twice B’s age. In 10 years, A will be 40. Find B’s present age.
Solution: A is currently 30. So, B is 15 years old.

Odd Man Out - Numbers

Definition: Identifying the element that does not fit a pattern.

Example: Find the odd one out: 3, 5, 7, 9, 11.
Solution: 9 is not a prime number. Answer: 9.

Distance and Directions

Definition: Problems based on the movement of objects/persons in different directions.

Example: A moves 5 km North, then 3 km East. Find the shortest distance.
Solution: Using the Pythagoras theorem, Answer: √34 km.

Statement & Conclusion

Definition: Logical reasoning based on provided statements.

Example: "All birds can fly." Does this mean penguins can fly?
Solution: No, the statement is not universally true.

Seating Arrangement

Definition: Arrangement of people in a row, circle, or grid.

Example: A, B, C, D sit in a row. B is left of C. Who is at the right end?
Solution: The order is A, B, C, D. Answer: D.

Analogy

Definition: Finding the relationship between two objects and applying the same logic to another pair.

Example: Doctor : Stethoscope :: Painter : ?
Solution: A doctor uses a stethoscope, and a painter uses a brush. Answer: Brush.

Mathematical Operations & Symbols

Definition: Understanding equations and symbols used in logical reasoning.

Example: If P * Q = P² + Q², find 3 * 4.
Solution: (3² + 4²) = 9 + 16 = 25. Answer: 25.
